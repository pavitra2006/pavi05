Technology

Technology refers to methods, systems, and devices which are the result of scientific knowledge being used for practical purposes.



Examples of Technlogy

2.Artifical Intelligence
3.Robotics

Information Technology

The four most basic and primary elements involving the use of all information technology include: information security, computer technical support, business software development and database and network management

Artifical Intelligence

The theory and development of computer systems able to perform tasks normally requiring human intelligence, such as visual perception, speech recognition, decision-making, and translation between languages.

Artificial intelligence (AI) is the intelligence of machines or software, as opposed to the intelligence of humans or animals. It is a field of study in computer science which develops and studies intelligent machines.
